Shrimp Scampi

A tasty summer hit!

Ingredients
  • 8 oz angel hair pasta
  • ½ cup butter
  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 lb cooked shrimp shelled and deveined
  • ½ cup white wine vinegar
  • ½ cup water
  • ¾ cup parmesan
  • ½ tablespoon oregano
  • 1 tablespoon parsley
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper
Instructions:
  1. Boil noodles in lightly salted water. Drain well!
  2. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat
  3. Saute minced garlic in butter until lightly browned
  4. Add water, vinegar, parsley, oregano and pepper and bring to a boil
  5. Reduce heat and add shrimp. Cook until warm up to 1 minute
  6. Toss drained noodles, shrimp, sauce and parmesan together
