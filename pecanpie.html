Pecan Pie

A sweet treat!

Ingredients:
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup dark corn syrup
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup chopped pecans
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 frozen pie shell
Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F
  2. Beat eggs thoroughly!
  3. Mix eggs, syrup, butter, sugar, pecans, vanilla, cinnamon together in a bowl
  4. Pour mixture into frozen pie shell
  5. Bake 325 degrees F 30 - 45 minutes until the edges are set and the center jiggles slightly
